## Weekly Workflow

Each week, you should:

1. **Before class:** Read the LESSON.md for the current topic
2. **In class:** Participate in demonstrations and in-class exercises
3. **After class:**
   - Work through the exercises (worked examples, Parsons problems, completion, practice)
   - Complete the mini-project
   - Review for any upcoming quizzes or exams
